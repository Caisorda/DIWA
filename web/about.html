DIWA

DIWA is a disaster management application having the primary function of informing its user about possible disasters, corresponding safety guidelines, and materials needed together with where you can get them. Target users of this application will be everyone but more specifically people living in disaster prone areas. The application begins with the location of the user or what location does the user wants to use through the use of maps and then the system shows all possible disaster warning such as floods, earthquakes, tsunamis, and more. Next, the system tells the safety guidelines for the corresponding disaster and what materials may be needed for preparation including near-by evacuation centers.
